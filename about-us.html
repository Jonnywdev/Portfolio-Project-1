Welcome to Mozz's

Mozz's are a leading North west based mobile catering company. Based in the Wigan borough, we provide event catering for a host of private and corporate clients across, Lancashire, Cheshire, and Yorkshire & Cumbria. Our work ethos is based on passion, perfection and attention to detail. Our executive chef Anthony Morris, has an extensive background in the catering industry, from working in top class restaurants to 5 star hotels across the UK. He possesses expert knowledge in all aspects of the catering sector, from banqueting to fine dining. Mozz's was established by Anthony Morris, who has spent the last 25 years learning and perfecting his skills. This is evident in the creative signature dishes he offers. We prepare all our food in our purpose built kitchen based in Pennington, Leigh which has a 5 star hygiene rating provided by Wigan Food Standards Agency.
